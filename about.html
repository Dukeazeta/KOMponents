About KOMponents

Our mission is to make Flutter development faster and more enjoyable

Our Story

KOMponents started as a collection of reusable widgets that we found ourselves creating repeatedly across different Flutter projects. We decided to package these components into a library to help other developers save time and maintain consistency in their applications.

Our focus is on creating components that are:

  • Highly customizable
  • Performance optimized
  • Well documented
  • Easy to integrate

Open Source

KOMponents is completely open source and free to use in your projects

Community Driven

Built with feedback and contributions from the Flutter community

Regular Updates

Continuous improvements and new components added regularly
